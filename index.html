About Me

I got my B.S. degree in Automation of Honors Program with Cum Laude from China Agricultural University, Beijing, China, in 2014.
After that, I received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from New York University, New York, NY, in 2017.
Since 2018, I have been at University of North Texas as a Ph.D student, working under the guidance of Dr. Tao Yang and Dr. Colleen Bailey.

In 2018 summer, I was working with Dr. Ye Yuan as a visiting scholar at Key Laboratory of Image Processing and Intelligent Control of Education Ministry, School of Artificial Intelligence and Automation, Huazhong University of Science and Technology.
I am also a member of USA Powerlifting. (Coached by David Garcia, a.k.a USAPL`s “The Predator”).

My research interests are in statistical learning, sparse PCA, machine learning, control theory, robotics, reinforcement learning, distributed optimization.
