About Me  —  [Short Bio]

I got my B.S. degree in Automation of Honors Program from China Agricultural University, Beijing, China, in 2014.
After that, I received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from New York University, New York, NY, in 2017.
Since 2018, I have been at Cyber-Physical Energy System Laboratory, University of North Texas as a Ph.D student, working under the guidance of Professor Tao Yang.
I am holding a visiting scholar position at Key Laboratory of Image Processing and Intelligent Control of Education Ministry, School of Automation, Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Supervised by: Professor Ye Yuan.
I am also a member of USA Powerlifting. (Coached by David Garcia, a.k.a USAPL`s “The Predator”).

My research interests are in control theory, robotics, statistical learning, machine learning, reinforcement learning, distributed optimization.
